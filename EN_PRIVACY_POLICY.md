# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information collected by Matris (the “App”) is processed. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

## 1) Information Collected

The App processes the following information to provide its functionality:

* Date of birth
* Time of birth
* Place of birth
* Gender (optional)
* Focus area and life situation (optional)

This information is used to generate numerology analyses.

## 2) Purpose of Use

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

* Generating personalized numerology matrices and analysis results
* Personalizing the app experience
* Diagnosing technical issues and improving the service

## 3) Third-Party Services

The App may use the following services:

* Firebase (authentication, cloud functions)
* RevenueCat (subscription management)

These services are subject to their own privacy policies.

## 4) Data Storage

User data may be stored locally on the device. Some information may also be processed via cloud services (e.g., for analysis generation). Data is retained only for as long as necessary.

## 5) Advertising and Tracking

The App does not use third-party tracking for advertising purposes.

## 6) Data Sharing

User data is never sold or marketed to third parties. Data is processed only to the extent necessary for the operation of the services.

## 7) Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. No data is knowingly collected from children under 13.

## 8) Security

Reasonable technical and administrative measures are taken to protect data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
